NRVA

NRVA is an Indonesian dark-alt project blending emotional storytelling, cinematic sound design, and haunting electronic textures. Rooted in themes of regret, memory, and fragile hope, NRVA creates music that feels intimate yet otherworldly—where painful truths and beautiful illusions collide.


One built from truths that cut, and one shaped from memories softened into dreams. Each song is a fragment lost between them. half real, half imagined, never fully belonging to either side.
